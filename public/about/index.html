About

Adam Hilton is a sound and technological artist and musician in Austin, TX. He composes and improvises works for theatre, dance, and context-responsive installations, blending abstract sound, interactive music technologies, and traditional music idioms. He has created in collaboration with COTFG, NMASS, BLiPSWiTCH, SoundSpace at the Blanton, Collide Arts, Phonography Austin, and Salvage Vanguard Theater among many others.
